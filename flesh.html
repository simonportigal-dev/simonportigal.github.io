Flesh-Nexus, Human Sword(wip)

Flesh-Nexus, Human Sword mixes movement meditations with the logical capacities of generative AI to construct movement scores - blending digital logic and somatic practice to explore interconnectedness and collective movement. Reimagining the space between us as a "virtual social fascia", Flesh-Nexus, Human Sword dances in the perverse threshold between physical and digital entanglement.
