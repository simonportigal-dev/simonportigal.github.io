fragile & useless (2022)

fragile & useless imagines a contemporary folk dance made in and of the era of hyperdigitization.

Artichaut Magazine

Concept, Direction, Choreography: Simon Portigal

Performance: Justin de Luna, Emile Pineault, Louise-Michel Jackson

Light Design: Nien-Tzu Weng

Digital Animation: Timothy Thomasson

Outside-eye/Rehearsal Direction: Sasha Kleinplatz

Stage Management: Michael Martini

Movement Research: Hanako Hoshimi-Caines, Nathan Yaffe, Winnie Ho, Scott McCabe, Zoe Vos, Paige Culley, Justin de Luna, Sovann Prom Tep, Louise-Michel Jackson, Emile Pineault

Thanks to Lara Oundjian, Sebastian Kann, and Dana Gingras
