Studies in Virtuality(wip)

By exerimentnig with forms of simulation in various media, Studies in Virtuality explores death and dying through a live experience of virtuality - treating ordinary immaterialities as insconspicuous windows to the afterlife.

Concept, Direction, Choreography: Simon Portigal

Collaborators: Justin de Luna, Jeremy Hervieux, Timothy Thomasson

This projected has currently benefitted from residencies at Parbleux and Studio 303.
