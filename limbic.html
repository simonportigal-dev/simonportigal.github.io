limbic cum rag (2015-2019)

An iterative performance, limbic cum rag uses the green-screen as a conceptual framework to explore how a performer's body becomes a site of projection - both literally and figuratively.

Concept, Direction, Choreography, Performance, Video, Sound Design: Simon Portigal

Light Design: Tim Rodrigues, Hugo Dalphond

Thanks to Ellen Furey, Dana Michel, Andrew Tay, Summerworks Performance Festival, OFFTA
